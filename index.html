Under the Hood

How IO Works

From first ring to booked appointment — in four seamless steps.

Step 01 — The Call

IO Picks Up Instantly

When someone calls your business, IO answers immediately with a natural, conversational voice. It identifies caller intent and begins qualifying within seconds — no hold music, no voicemail.

Step 02 — Qualification

Smart Lead Qualification

IO asks targeted questions tailored to your business — budget, timeline, service needs. It separates hot leads from tire-kickers, so only qualified prospects reach your team.

Step 03 — Follow-Up

Seamless Text Follow-Ups

After the call, IO transitions to SMS. It sends personalized follow-ups and continues the sales conversation — at exactly the right moment, every time.

Step 04 — Book & Report

Appointments & Analytics

IO books appointments directly into your calendar and compiles full analytics — transcripts, lead scores, next steps. You wake up to a full pipeline and zero effort.
